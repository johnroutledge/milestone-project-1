Visa Help

Emigrating to the UK from countries outside of Europe, such as Thailand, can be a very complex and confusing process. To help you navigate your way through all the documentation, we have provided links to the key pages from the UK.GOV website.

We add regular updates to the visa process as and when they occur, so be sure to check back with us regularly to get the latest help and tips for your visa application.

NHS Surcharge

Access to the halthcare system (the NHS) in the UK is free of charge to its citizens. Before you have stayed long enough to obtain UK citizenship, a charge must be paid at each stage of the visa process to enable you access to this service.
