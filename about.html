About the Bilingual Boys

Tam and John moved to the UK in 2016, having met in Thailand five years earlier. The twins were born a year later in 2017 and speak both Thai and English. Tam will be applying for her 'indefinitive leave to remain' visa later this year, followed by UK citizenship.

Having been through the UK visa application process right from the beginning in Thailand, they have a sound understanding and knowledge of what is involved at each stage: from form completion to document sourcing, timelines and deadlines.
